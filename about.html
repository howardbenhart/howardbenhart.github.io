What is Formula One

Formula One (F1) is the highest level of international motorsport, featuring single-seater racing cars competing in a series of Grand Prix races held worldwide. It is known for its cutting-edge technology, intense competition, and global fanbase, making it a pinnacle of both sport and engineering.

Why Formula One

Formula One offers a thrilling mix of speed, strategy, and cutting-edge technology, with each race showcasing incredible driver skill and intense team competition. The excitement of unpredictable moments, dramatic overtakes, and the roar of powerful engines makes every Grand Prix an unforgettable experience.

Teams

Formula 1 currently features 10 teams, each with two drivers, making up a 20-driver grid. These teams include iconic names like Red Bull Racing, Ferrari, and Mercedes, along with others such as McLaren, Aston Martin, Alpine, Haas, Williams, Sauber (now Stake F1), and AlphaTauri (rebranded to Visa Cash App RB)

Current World Champion

The current Formula 1 World Champion is Max Verstappen, who secured his fourth consecutive title during the 2024 season.
