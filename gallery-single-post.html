Drivers

Formula 1 drivers are elite athletes who compete at the highest level of motorsport, piloting advanced single-seater cars at incredible speeds. They possess exceptional skill, quick reflexes, and strategic thinking, starting their careers in karting and progressing through junior categories. Drivers work closely with engineers to optimize car performance, provide feedback, and execute race strategies. Each team has two drivers, often pairing experience with youth.
